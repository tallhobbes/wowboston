By mapping and filtering the Boston property assessment dataset , you can use a simple relationship - Who Owns What? - to explore and discover many interesting patterns otherwise buried.
Choose filters for your data
Select which parts to examine
Subset data to your selection for finer detail
Flying around the map of your selections, you can explore things like:
The City of Boston owns lots of property: can you find what types and where? What do these trends tell you?
How expensive are residential accommodations in different parts of town, and where does the money go? What is the best value?
Are a region's commercial buildings owned by local, national, or international companies? How do those values compare?
Does Boston have areas that are more likely to pay rent vs. more likely to receive it?
This tool is designed to be a fascinating way to find some things that challenge your expectations, and discover other things you may have never even considered!
